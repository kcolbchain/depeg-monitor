Live prices from Binance, Coinbase, CoinGecko, and Jupiter (Solana DEX aggregator). REST cadence: CEXes at the configured interval (default 5s), CoinGecko at 20s, Jupiter at 5s. Toggle stream to WebSocket to switch Binance and Coinbase to public WS feeds for sub-second updates. Historical via CoinGecko's /coins/{id}/market_chart (auto-granularity ≤1d 5-min, ≤90d hourly, >90d daily). Each observation is exposed at window.depegMonitor.lastObservations in the depeg-oracles wire format. The full depeg-monitor library runs server-side with direct Uniswap V3 + Curve reads.
